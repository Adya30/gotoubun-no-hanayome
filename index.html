Gotoubun No Hanayome

One day during his lunch break, Uesugi argues with a female transfer student who has claimed "his seat," leading both of them to dislike each other. That same day, he is presented with a golden opportunity to clear his family's debt: a private tutoring gig for a wealthy family's daughter, with a wage of five times the market price. He accepts the proposal, but is horrified to discover that the client, Itsuki Nakano, is the girl he confronted earlier!

Fuutarou Uesugi

Fuutarou is a hardworking and intelligent student who comes from a poor family. He is blunt, socially awkward, and initially insensitive to others feelings. His main motivation is to earn money to support his family, which leads him to become the tutor of the Nakano quintuplets. Over time, Fuutarou learns the importance of empathy, emotional connection, and trust. His growth from a cold, goal-oriented person into someone who genuinely cares about others is central to the story.

Nino Nakano

Nino is bold, fiery, and extremely straightforward. She has a strong sense of family loyalty and is very protective of her sisters, often acting aggressively toward anyone she sees as a threat. At the beginning, she openly hates Fuutarou and tries to drive him away. However, once she accepts him, she falls deeply in love and becomes fiercely honest about her feelings. Nino values sincerity above all else and despises dishonesty, making her one of the most emotionally intense characters in the series.

Ichika Nakano

Ichika is the eldest of the Nakano quintuplets and often acts like the “big sister” of the group. She has a playful, teasing personality and usually maintains a relaxed and cheerful attitude. As an aspiring actress, Ichika is skilled at reading situations and people, which makes her emotionally intelligent but also somewhat manipulative at times. Although she appears carefree, she struggles internally with insecurity and fear of being left behind, especially when it comes to love. Her actions are driven by a mix of selflessness and selfish desire.

Itsuki Nakano

Itsuki is serious, diligent, and highly responsible. She values discipline and education and dreams of becoming a teacher like her mother. Although she often clashes with Fuutarou due to their similar stubborn personalities, she gradually develops respect and trust toward him. Itsuki often acts as the moral compass of the group and tries to maintain balance among her sisters. She represents stability, hard work, and a strong sense of duty.
